Ja'Mesha is a student at Operation Spark- a program that gives teens and young adults the tools needed to become programmers. A native of New Orleans, she earned a bachelor's degree in Communications from Dillard University. After graduation, she interned at Entergy Corporation in Corporate Communications. It was then that she decided to pursue a different career. Ja'Mesha wanted to put her love of technology to use and become a software engineer.

Her long-term goal is to acquire the experience and knowledge necessary to become a data scientist, in addition to doing her part to aid the growing demand of programmers by following Operation Spark's example and teach others.

In her spare time, she volunteers in the community with Harrah's Hero program as well as the Young Leadership Council of New Orleans. She's a hard worker and team player who's excited about her future.
